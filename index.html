Hi I'm Sourabh Somani

Lead Developer, Microsoft MVP, C# Corner MVP

Sourabh Somani, the 3 times Microsoft MVP and 6-times C# Corner MVP, is a tech lead, contributor, International Speaker, Author, and the founder of www.PythonBabu.com. After possessing a B-Tech degree in Computer Science, he found his niche in core programming and database development. His core competencies include Mobile and web applications development using ASP.NET, Python, MVC, Node.js, C#, Java Script, jQuery, SQL Server, NoSQL, MongoDB, and Angular. With his extensive experience of several years, passion toward learning new technologies, and a zeal to give back to the developer community, Sourabh contributes his knowledge via very helpful articles and blogs on various community sites.
